About Word of Testimony...

The Bible Says in Rev 12:9 KJV  And the great dragon was cast out, that old serpent, called the Devil, and Satan, which deceiveth the whole world:
he was cast out into the earth, and his angels were cast out with him. 
Rev 12:10 KJV  And I heard a loud voice saying in heaven, Now is come salvation, and strength, and the kingdom of our God, and the power of his Christ:
for the accuser of our brethren is cast down, which accused them before our God day and night. 

Who is this Accuser of the Brethren as mentioned in verse 10? He is none other then Satan Himself. You might say "how is that"? Who else is it that accuses you,
and tells you lies day and night? Who else deceives the whole world? The devil. Is there any hope then, seeing that Satan's influence is springing up before God
and his people, and he is attacking the Bride of Jesus Christ with his lies?

Yes, there is hope. We cant stop there, there is more in Rev 12:11 KJV And they overcame him by the blood of the Lamb, and by the word of their testimony; and they loved not their lives unto the death.

Who does it mean by they? YOU! You are the one who will overcome the devil with the blood of the Lamb and the word of your testimony. You have
the option to either bring a testimony Against the devil and claim the Blood of Jesus Christ in your life! Or you can accept the devils lie and bring a
testimony against God. Of course your testimony against God will not hinder God in any way, it will only hinder you!

Our goal is to bring a testimony(or witness)Against the devil, especially in this hour because we see all of the devils worldly influence today. So we as
Christians can bring a testimony against that simply because we are saved by nothing else but the Blood of Jesus Christ. That is all you need to bring
to the devil as a witness that you are not bound to the devil. You are a child of God. May the Lord help us as we provide Godly testimony for the Word of God
and the Blood that He shed on calvary. God Bless You!
